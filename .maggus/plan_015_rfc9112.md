# Plan: RFC 9112 (HTTP/1.1) — 99% Client Compliance

## Introduction

RFC 9112 has 82 client-side MUSTs. After plan_011 (TASK-010-013), 80 are covered (98%). This plan closes the last partials to reach 99%.

## Goals

- RFC 9112 client compliance from 91.5% to 99% (81/82)
- Upgrade all 7 PARTIALLY implemented items to FULLY
- Remaining 1 item: TLS closure alert verification (delegated to framework)

## Preconditions

- **TE + Connection**: `Http11Encoder` auto-adds "TE" to Connection header when TE header is present
- **Pipeline reconnect guard**: `Http1XCorrelationStage` has `_isFirstRequestAfterReconnect` flag preventing pipelining after reconnect
- **TLS closure detection**: `ClientByteMover` distinguishes clean TLS close from abrupt TCP close
- **Incomplete close recovery**: Chunked without zero-chunk and CL-truncated responses are marked incomplete
